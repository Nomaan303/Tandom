# Tandom
Boost Your Productivity with Tandem – A Multitasking Mini Browser for Chrome & Edge

Tandem is a mini browser sidebar designed to help you stay productive without leaving your current page. Whether you're working on a project, browsing, or multitasking, Tandem lets you view and interact with websites directly from a side panel—all without switching tabs. Perfect for remote teams, marketers, and anyone who needs to keep multiple tasks open at once.
