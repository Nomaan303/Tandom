## üöÄ Introduction

**Tandem** is a powerful mini sidebar extension designed to streamline your browsing workflow. Whether you are a developer checking responsive designs, a student taking notes while watching a lecture, or just someone who loves efficiency, Tandem allows you to view two websites simultaneously without constantly switching tabs.

## ‚ú® Key Features

* **Seamless Sidebar:** Open a secondary browsing window inside your current tab.
* **True Multitasking:** Watch videos, read articles, or check social media while working on your main screen.
* **Lightweight & Fast:** Designed to be minimal and unobtrusive, ensuring your browser stays fast.
* **Quick Toggle:** Easily open and close the sidebar with a single click.
